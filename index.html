PAST WORK

At the end of 2022, I completed my year 13 design board. My brief for this board centered around creating branding, advertisement, packaging, and products for a community garden.

The aim of this brand was to encourage more people from a variety of demographics to engage in community gardening due to all of the benefits it achieve. Including mental and physical well-being. As well as the sense of connection/community it can provide individuals.

I started off creating a brand identity by investigating the use of different type, image and logos.

I then created a range of products to go into a starter gardening kit, such as an apron, gardening tools, gloves, seed packets, and starter seed pots. I also designed posters and a community newsletter.

CURRENT WORK

At the moment I’m taking the studio course Lens - a photography-based course.

The brief we are currently working towards is responding to concepts surrounding ‘Mihimihi’ looking to answer questions such as Who are you? Who are your people? Where do you come from? etc…

I have chosen to take portraits of my immediate family as well as use portraits of my ancestors to respond to this brief.

I’ve used elements like handwriting and fingerprints as these are closely tied to ideas of identity and individuality. Introducing my family members using their own handwriting and thoughts.
